About Me

Hello there! My full name is Tryz Daron C. Odasco, but you can call me Daron. I was born on October 28, 2003. As a child, I was always curious about how things work. I often took apart my robotic toys — sometimes I managed to put them back together, but most of the time I ended up building something completely new from their parts. Over time, I also developed an interest in computers and games. I’ve always liked being outdoors, and when I got my first motorcycle, I rode it every single day for a month. My curiosity didn’t stop there — I started learning how motorcycles work, bought some tools, and before long I was repairing my own bike, and even helping fix other people’s motorcycles too.
